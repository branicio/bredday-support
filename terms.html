2. Description of Service

BredDay is a livestock gestation and breeding record-keeping tool that provides:

  • Estimated due-date calculation from breeding dates for cattle, horses, goats, sheep, pigs, rabbits, alpacas, and llamas
  • Per-animal breeding logs, checkpoints, and outcome records
  • A quick gestation calculator (Quick Calc)
  • Calendar view of due dates and checkpoints
  • Optional local checkpoint reminders
  • A home-screen widget with upcoming due dates
  • CSV export of your herd log
