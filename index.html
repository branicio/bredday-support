What BredDay does

Everything you need from breeding to birth

One tidy app for the whole cycle — figure the due date, keep the herd, and never miss a checkpoint, all without a signal.

📅

Due dates you can trust

Enter the breeding date and get the estimated due date instantly — with breed-specific gestation lengths and a per-animal override when you know your stock better than any table.

🐮

Your whole herd, in order

Log every animal, track breeding history and outcomes (live & lost), and see who's closest to due at a glance — sorted and color-coded by how soon.

New
✅

Checkpoints & reminders

Vet-informed checkpoint templates for every species — plus your own custom checkpoints to match how your farm actually runs, each with an optional local reminder.

🗓️

Calendar view

Every due date and checkpoint laid out on a month calendar, so nothing sneaks up on you mid-season.

📲

Home-screen widget

A countdown right on your home screen — “Bessie · 14d” — and the next three coming up. Glance, don't dig.

⚡

Quick Calc

Need a fast answer at the chute? Pick a species and a date — due date on the spot, nothing to save.

🔒

Private by design — and it works in the barn

100% offline: no signal, no problem, and no account to create. No ads, no analytics, no tracking — your herd data never leaves your phone (the App Store label reads Data Not Collected). Export the whole herd to CSV any time it needs to go elsewhere.
